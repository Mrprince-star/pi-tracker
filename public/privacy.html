Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What we collect

Tracker for Pioneers stores the figures you manually enter — balances, mining stats, role progress, and checklist completion — locally on your own device, so you don't have to re-enter them every visit. If you choose to connect with Pi, we receive only your Pi username, for personalization, as permitted by the Pi SDK's username scope.

What we never collect

We never request, receive, or store your passphrase, private keys, or any wallet credentials. We have no technical ability to access your actual Pi balance, KYC status, or account status, regardless of what you enter into Tracker for Pioneers.

Payments

Optional tips are processed entirely through the Pi Wallet via the Pi SDK's payments scope. Tracker for Pioneers never handles your payment credentials directly, and cannot initiate a payment without your explicit on-screen confirmation through the Pi Wallet itself. A record that your Pi account has tipped is stored on our servers so unlocked features stay unlocked across sessions and devices; no payment or wallet details are stored alongside this record, only your Pi user ID and a yes/no flag.

Advertising

Tracker for Pioneers is not currently part of the Pi Ad Network, and does not currently display ads from any source. If that changes in the future, ads may be delivered through Pi's own Ad Network, or through a reputable third-party advertising network, to help cover the costs of running and improving this app. If third-party ads are ever introduced, this policy will be updated first, and any such network's own privacy practices would apply to the ads themselves, separate from the data you enter into Tracker for Pioneers. We will not sell your entered balance data to any advertiser.

Third parties

Beyond the possibility of advertising described above, we do not sell, rent, or share your entered data with any third party.

Data retention

Locally stored data (your entered balances and tool inputs) remains on your device until you clear it. Server-stored data (your tip status, tied to your Pi user ID) is retained for as long as your account uses this app, and can be deleted on request by contacting us.

Your control

You can clear your entered data at any time by clearing the app's local storage in your browser or device settings. Since most data is stored on your device, clearing site data or uninstalling removes it completely. To request deletion of server-stored tip status, contact us using the details below.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and, for material changes, note them clearly in the app. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated terms.

Contact

Questions about this policy, or requests to delete server-stored data, can be sent to faithfulbizteam@gmail.com.
